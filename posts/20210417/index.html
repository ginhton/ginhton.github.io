feeling

Let’s talk about feeling first. I have experienced fear, ecstasy, despairation, sadness and overwhelmed. Cannot keep a peaceful mind any more. Just because I want something. But pursuing something is right and we should do that right? Then why should I suffer those extreme feelings? Those feelings are just feelings that affect my mind, but have no good effect with the result of the specific things.

Can I stay away from those feelings and only focus on what should I do?

Maybe I can. But what is the first step, and next?

Well, the first is to do something. Anything is ok. Next, do the right thing, the thing that I should do or am supposed to do. Then, ignore feelings, and they will go with wind.
