  • webContents is an EventEmitter. it is responsible for rendering and controlling a web page and is a property of the BrowserWindow object. So there are many events callback can bind on this object.
  • contents.loadURL(url[, options]): loads the url in the window. The url must contain the protocol prefix, e.g. the http:// or file://.
  • BrowserWindow creates and controls browser windows. It has on('close', callback), show, hide, loadURL(url[,options]) and other functions.
  • Array.shift will pop out the first item in array.
  • Template literals are enclosed by the back-tick(` `) character instead of double or single quotes. Template literals can contain placeholders. These are indicated by the dollar sign and curly braces ${expression}.

  • Create a frameless window

  • Frameless window is non-draggable. Apps need to specify -webkit-app-region: drag in CSS to tell Electron which regions are draggable, and apps can use -webkit-app-region: no-drag to exclude the non-draggable area from the draggable region. To make the whole window draggable, you can add -webkit-app-region: drag as body\’s style.

  • use window.events.emit('custom-event') to emit event and use window.events.on('custom-event', cb) to do something when custom-event happens.

  • The repository I will learn today is ffmpeg-libav-tutorial.
  • The basic idea behind a video is a series of pictures / frames running at a given rate.
  • CODEC is an electronic circuit or software that compresses or decompresses digital audio/video.
  • .webm file havs vp9 and opus sections. .mp4 file has h264 and aac sections.
  • Transcoding is the act of converting one of the streams (audio or video) from one CODEC to another one.
  • TransMuxing is the act of converting from one format (container) to another one.
  • Transrating is the act of changing the bit rate, or producint other renditions.
  • Transsizing is the act of converting from one resolution to another one. As said before transsizing is often used with transrating.
  • When we are designing a video player we need to play each frame at a given pace. Each frame has a presentation timestamp (PTS) which is an increasing number factored in a timebase that is a ration number (where the denominator is known as timescale) divisilbe by the frame rate (fps).

mayBeYouAreInDepression

发表于 2018-01-20

2 Copy

  • I find this answer in Quora.

  • If you desire nothing, you shouldn’t have wished to know the cause behind this state of your mind and wouldn’t have posted this question here.

  • Do a simple experiment. I will assume that you have desire for NOTHING. Lock yourself in a room. Don’t come out for two days. switch off your phone. Don’t let anyone disturb you by any means. Don’t either eat. Can you do this?

  • It’s not absurd or weired to be in such situation. A person who is damn overloaded to complete this professional duties within deadlines finds no joy in doing anything once he/she finds a break. He/she seeks idleness.

  • Those, who have lost almost a good chunk of their life (a complete loss of material assets or entire family), keep on dragging themselves. Can you find any reason behind this emptiness.

  • Its my personal perception that when you are at lowest, when you are completely blank, when you lose desire or affection towards everything, go to the most saddest place nearby you. Go to a nearby slum, an orphanage or any other ruined place which once used to flourish.

  • What you will find there? Even if these places are filled with utmost uncertainly, its still filled with a life force. A desire to survive & exist. These people have faintest of the idea on how to survive. But still they desire it.

  • Don’t look for any proven methodology to regain or transform yourself. Nothing of such crap exists. Just do a little in depth introspection. Look for which color of life suites you.

  • You are not a robot/machine who can live a gray life forever. Starts slowly over nothing but something randomly. Sometimes, answers are with us, we just turn a blind eye to them forcefully.
